Quote

Here is another quote to remind all of us that everyone is fighting the battle called, "Life." In buddism there is a saying,

"Suffering exists. Suffering arises from attachments to desires.
Suffering ceases when attachment to desire ceases.
Freedom from suffering is possible."

These two quotes are intermingled in that we should be mindful of the suffering of others and how it is universal. When someone is angry or lashes out at you, remain detached and understand they may just be having a bad day.
